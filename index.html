Profile

Research that turns speech models into learner-facing systems.

My work connects automatic speech recognition, spoken language assessment, and computer-assisted language learning. The common thread is usable feedback: systems that can help learners, teachers, and researchers even when task-specific data is limited.

Current applied work covers Finland Swedish mispronunciation detection, Whisper-based automatic speaking assessment, Finnish L2 content feedback, and mobile learning apps such as SaySuomi, SaySvenska, and Pop2TalkNordic.

Research

Core directions

A focused research agenda across speech modeling, feedback design, and language learning applications.

01

Automatic speaking assessment

End-to-end scoring for spoken responses using compact speech encoders and lightweight aggregation.

02

Pronunciation feedback

Mispronunciation detection and feedback methods for learners in low-resource language settings.

03

Learning applications

Speech-enabled mobile tools for pronunciation practice, task completion, and self-study feedback.

04

Low-resource speech AI

Robust modeling strategies for Finnish, Finland Swedish, and other under-supported tasks.
